    🏋️ Weight Gain & Recovery Plan#

    Target: Recover to 75 kg (from 64 kg) Daily Target: ~2,900 Calories | ~140g Protein

    🛒 2. Shopping Lists (No Freezer Strategy)#

    Shopping is split twice a week to keep meat and produce fresh.

    🟢 Trip A: The 3-Day Haul (e.g., Mon–Wed)#

    Buy this on Day 1 to cover Days 1, 2, and 3.

    Produce

    • Bananas: 3 large
    • Apples: 3
    • Potatoes: 1.2 kg

    Meat & Dairy

    • Ground Beef or Chicken Thighs: 500g
    • Eggs: 10 (or 1 dozen box)
    • Whole Milk: 2 Liters

    Pantry & Grains

    • Pasta or Rice: 500g pack
    • Mixed Veggies: 450g (Fresh or Canned)
    • Nuts: 1 bag (~200g)

    🔵 Trip B: The 4-Day Haul (e.g., Thu–Sun)#

    Buy this on Day 4 to cover Days 4, 5, 6, and 7.

    Produce

    • Bananas: 4 large
    • Apples: 4
    • Potatoes: 1.6 kg

    Meat & Dairy

    • Ground Beef or Chicken Thighs: 600g (Family pack size)
    • Eggs: 12 (1 dozen box)
    • Whole Milk: 3 Liters

    Pantry & Grains

    • Pasta or Rice: 600g
    • Mixed Veggies: 600g
    • Nuts: 1 bag (~250g)

    🏠 3. Monthly Staples#

    Check these before you leave the house; buy only when running low.

    • Oats (Instant or Rolled)
    • Peanut Butter
    • Olive Oil
    • Butter
    • Sauces (Pesto, Tomato, Hot Sauce, etc.)
    • Whey Protein (Optional backup)

    ⚡ 4. Rules for Success#

    1. Batch Cook Lunch: Cook your rice/pasta and meat for 2 days at a time. Store the second portion in the fridge. This ensures you never skip a meal because you are “too tired to cook.”
    2. Drink Your Calories: If you struggle to finish a meal, rely on the liquids (Milk and Shake). It is physically easier to drink 200 calories than to chew them.
    3. Gym Timing:
      • Eat Lunch 2–3 hours before training.
      • Have the Shake or Dinner immediately after training.
