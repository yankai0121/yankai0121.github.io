    When Can You Retire

    February 3, 2026

    4% rule: save 25 times your expected spending in your first year of retirement. Spending increase by 3% each year. – relatively conservative

    The crossover point rule: When your monthly investment income exceeds your monthly expenses.

    Crossover assets = Monthly expenses * Monthly investment return
